🌸 About Me

Hi, I’m Sophia! A storyboard artist and 2D animator focused on character-driven storytelling, expressive acting, and emotionally grounded visuals.

I’m especially drawn to moments where subtle expressions, pacing, and composition carry a scene. My work often explores relationships and emotional intimacy, with an emphasis on making characters feel natural, specific, and real.

I studied 3D & VFX Animation at the School of Visual Arts in New York City before transferring to the University of Central Florida, where I now study Emerging Media.

Currently, I’m developing my voice as a visual storyteller, with a focus on character performance and narrative clarity.
